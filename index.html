Hello!


My name is Younus, and what you are looking at is my personal endeavor to make something both creative and representative of myself as a prospective web developer, and as a a programmer.

Everything enclosed within this work was a labor of sweat, blood, and tears. More so, it was a true labor of love. The whole overall purpose of this site was to challenge myself as a programmer.
